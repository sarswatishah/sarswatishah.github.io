Currently, I am working as a DGAPA postdoctoral fellow in the Institute of Mathematics (IM), at the National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM), working with Prof. Gerardo Hernandez Dueñas. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Mathematics, at the in University of Delhi,, under the supervision of Prof. Randheer Singh. My research interests are focused on the theoretical and numerical aspects of non-linear hyperbolic partial differential equations.

  • Geophysical Fluid Dynamics and Atmospheric Sciences
